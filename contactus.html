Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trip?

Booking a trip is easy! Simply browse our trips page, select your desired destination, and click "Book Now". You'll be guided through our secure booking process where you can choose your travel dates and payment options.

What is included in the trip price?

Our trip prices typically include accommodation, transportation as specified in the itinerary, guided tours, and some meals. Each trip description provides a detailed breakdown of what's included. Additional activities or personal expenses are usually not covered.

Can I customize my trip?

Absolutely! We offer customizable options for most of our trips. Contact our travel specialists with your preferences, and we'll work with you to create a personalized itinerary that meets your needs and interests.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the trip and how close to the departure date you cancel. Generally, cancellations made 60+ days in advance receive a full refund, while those made closer to the departure date may incur fees. Specific details are provided during the booking process.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes, we partner with leading travel insurance providers to offer comprehensive coverage for your trip. You can add travel insurance during the booking process or contact us for more information about coverage options.
